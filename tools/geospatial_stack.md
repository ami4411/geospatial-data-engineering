# Modern Geospatial Tooling

## Where tools tend to fit

### GeoPandas
Excellent for exploratory vector analysis, feature engineering, prototyping, and moderate-sized analytical tasks.

### PostGIS
Best once spatial logic needs to be repeatable, queryable, governed, or shared across users and services.

### DuckDB
Very useful for analytical workflows, local experimentation, and SQL-style exploration of columnar spatial data.

### Rasterio and GDAL
Still essential when dealing seriously with raster ingestion, transformation, and export.

### PySAL
Helpful once the work moves into spatial statistics, autocorrelation, or regionalisation.

The mature geospatial stack is not about picking one winner. It is about combining the right tools for the shape of the problem.
